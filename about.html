About FLORAYNE



Welcome to Florayne — a floral boutique blooming in the heart of Kyiv.



Since our founding in 2021, we've been crafting elegant and modern floral compositions for every occasion — from everyday joy to unforgettable events. Each bouquet is thoughtfully created by our designers to express emotion, style, and timeless beauty.


At Florayne, we believe flowers speak the language of the soul. Whether it's a spontaneous gesture or a meaningful celebration, our mission is to help you say it with flowers — beautifully and effortlessly.



We offer:

Fresh seasonal bouquets and arrangements

Flower accessories and decor

Custom designs for weddings, events, and photoshoots

Same-day delivery within Kyiv



Visit our boutique or order online — and let your story bloom with us.
